Why Columba

No Internet Needed

Works without cell towers, servers, or internet. Your messages find their way through the mesh.

Encrypted by Default

End-to-end encryption on every message. No accounts, no tracking, no central servers.

Multiple Transports

Bluetooth LE for nearby, WiFi at home, LoRa via RNode for distance, or TCP to reach the world.

Voice Calls

Make encrypted voice calls right over the mesh network. No SIM card or phone number required.

Your Identity, Your Device

Cryptographic identity generated on your device. Share via QR code. No phone number needed.

Open Source

MIT licensed and fully auditable. Inspect the code, contribute, or fork it yourself.

How It Works

Create Identity

Generate a cryptographic identity on your device in seconds. No email, no phone number.

Connect

Enable Bluetooth, WiFi, or pair an RNode. Columba discovers peers automatically.

Message

Send encrypted messages that hop across the mesh until they reach their destination.

Connect Your Way

Bluetooth LE

~100m range

Connect to anyone nearby, anywhere. No infrastructure needed.

WiFi

Home & local

Use your existing WiFi network for faster local mesh connections.

LoRa / RNode

Up to 20+ km

Long-range radio with an RNode device. Reach across mountains and valleys.

TCP / Internet

Global reach

Connect to any Reticulum transport node worldwide when internet is available.
